Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

At Paybuntu, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and demo services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as:

  • Name and contact information (e.g., email address)
  • Authentication credentials (passwords are encrypted via Firebase)
  • Profile information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our demo services
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To personalize your experience within the demo application
  • To improve our demo services based on usage patterns

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Authentication and data storage are handled by Google Firebase, providing robust security for your credentials and information.

4. Third-Party Services

This demo uses Google Firebase for authentication and database services. Your data is subject to Google's privacy policies in the context of these infrastructure services.

5. Your Rights

Since this is a demo project, you can delete your account data at any time by contacting the developer or through the account management features provided within the app.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@paybuntu.demo.
